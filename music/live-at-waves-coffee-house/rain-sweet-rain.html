Rain, Sweet Rain
(Song for Fort McMurray)

Listen with YouTube

Rain, sweet cooling rain,
Come and fall on our burning land.
Quench our forests; fill our streams
Give us back our broken dreams.
Rain, won’t you bring us what you can.
Rain, sweet rain.

Pain, we feel the pain,
Hearts are hurting burnt by flame.
Can’t go back home for many a day.
Just can’t seem to find a way.
Rain, come and give a helping hand
Rain, sweet rain

C’mon and Rain! Rain! Rain
Rain! Rain! Rain
Rain! Rain! Rain
Rain! Rain! Rain

Hope, we pray for hope
As we rebuild our land.
Keep us safe, keep us strong;
Lift our spirits, as we sing this song.
While our nation helps us make a stand.
Rain, sweet rain.

Rain! Rain! Rain
Rain! Rain! Rain
Rain! Rain! Rain
Rain! Rain! Rain

Rain! Rain! Rain
Rain! Rain! Rain
Rain! Rain! Rain
Rain! Rain! Rain
