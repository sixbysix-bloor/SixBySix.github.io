When I Call Up To Jesus

Listen with YouTube

When I call up to Jesus
He comes running to me.
When I give Him my sorrows,
Hand Him my misery;
When I cry up to heaven
And give Him all my despair
I know that He’ll answer,
He’ll always be there.

CHORUS
So come all who are weary
And burdened in soul.
Because Jesus will lift you
If you give Him control.
Just surrender to Jesus,
Hand over your life.
He will take all your sorrow
And trouble and strife.

So pour out your heartaches
And let out your tears.
Just give Him your torment,
Hand Him all of your fears.
There is no kind of trouble
Too great or too small;
Just give them to Jesus,
He’ll handle them all.
